Top Contributors

The top supporters in the crypto community.

These individuals have made significant contributions to the development and popularization of blockchain technology:

Notable Contributors

  • Satoshi Nakamoto

    Known as the creator of Bitcoin and the underlying blockchain technology, Satoshi Nakamoto's work has revolutionized the financial world and paved the way for decentralized currencies.

  • Vitalik Buterin

    Co-founder of Ethereum, Vitalik Buterin has been instrumental in developing the Ethereum blockchain, which supports smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps).

  • Charlie Lee

    Creator of Litecoin, Charlie Lee designed this cryptocurrency to be the "silver to Bitcoin's gold," offering faster transaction times and a more scalable network.

  • Gavin Wood

    Co-founder of Ethereum and creator of Polkadot, Gavin Wood has contributed significantly to the advancement of blockchain interoperability and scalability.

  • Andreas M. Antonopoulos

    A prominent advocate for Bitcoin and open blockchain technologies, Andreas M. Antonopoulos is known for his educational books, talks, and efforts to spread awareness and understanding of cryptocurrency.

Top Startups

Leading innovators in the blockchain ecosystem.

These startups are making significant strides in the world of decentralized finance and crowdfunding:

Notable Startups

  • DAO Maker

    Specializes in tokenized crowdfunding solutions and DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) creation, aiming to facilitate fundraising and community engagement through token sales.

  • Polkastarter

    Operates as a decentralized fundraising platform built for cross-chain token pools and auctions, allowing projects to raise capital and achieve liquidity.

  • KickICO

    Provides a platform for crowdfunding campaigns based on blockchain technology, enabling projects to raise funds through token sales.

  • TrustSwap

    Offers a suite of decentralized finance (DeFi) solutions, including smart contract-based services for fundraising, escrow, and token swaps.

  • Molecule

    Focuses on tokenizing intellectual property and scientific research through decentralized crowdfunding, enabling direct participation and investment in scientific discoveries.
